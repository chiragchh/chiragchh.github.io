Chirag Chhablani

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Illinois, Chicago , working under the supervision of Dr. Ian A. Kash . My work is broadly focused on applied sequential decision making and explainable AI.

I received B.E.(Hons.) in Electrical and Electronics and M.E (Hons.) in Software Systems from Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani, where I was advised by Professor Kuldeep Garg. I was also a research fellow at School of Information systems.
